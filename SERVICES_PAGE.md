## Page Overview

The Services page details Inbound Impact's three core offerings — HubSpot implementation, custom website development, and SEO. Each service is presented with a clear description and a checklist of deliverables so prospects understand exactly what they're getting.

**Primary objectives:**
- Clearly communicate the scope and depth of each service
- Establish technical credibility through specificity (Hub names, tiers, methodologies)
- Give prospects enough information to self-qualify before reaching out
- Drive conversions to the contact page

## Design System Notes

- **Layout:** 2-column card grid on desktop, single column on mobile
- **Service cards:** Glassmorphic borders, icon with hover transition (outline → filled orange), checklist with orange check icons
- **Typography:** Manrope (headings), Geist Sans (body)
- **Brand colours:** Orange `#FF6600` (primary), Yellow gradient accent
- **Theme support:** Full light/dark mode
- **Animations:** GSAP staggered scroll-reveal for cards
