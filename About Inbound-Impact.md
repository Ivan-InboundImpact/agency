# Inbound Impact

Boutique HubSpot agency. We handle onboarding, custom websites, and SEO — all inside the HubSpot ecosystem.

## The short version

We got tired of seeing HubSpot projects done badly — cookie-cutter setups, bloated retainers, junior staff doing the actual work. So we built something different.

Inbound Impact is a small team that specialises exclusively in HubSpot. Onboarding and implementation, custom website development on HubSpot CMS, and on-page SEO. We work across Marketing, Sales, Service, and Operations Hubs — Pro and Enterprise.

Every project is handled directly by founders and senior people. No account managers. No handoffs.
